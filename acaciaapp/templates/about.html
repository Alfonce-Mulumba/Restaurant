Meet Our Executive Chef

Bringing world-class flavours to Acacia Resort

At Acacia Resort, our culinary philosophy is deeply rooted in authentic African hospitality and world-inspired creativity. Chef Emmanuel Karanja leads our kitchen with a passion for fresh, locally sourced ingredients and refined cooking techniques that highlight Kenya’s rich food heritage.

With decades of experience in luxury resorts across East Africa, Chef Emmanuel has mastered the art of blending traditional flavours with modern presentation—creating memorable dining experiences for guests from around the world.
